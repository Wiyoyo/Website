ABOUT
Hi! I'm Willem West, a passionate 3D animator and multidisciplinary designer currently based in Colorado Springs, Colorado. Originally, I was born in Leicester, England, and later moved to the States when I was young.

I create a variety of 3D animations, concept ideas, and design work. Currently, I am a student at the University of Colorado Colorado Springs where I studied cyber security for 2 years and am now studying digital marketing.

I have worked with multiple clients to create animations and concept designs for brand and client promotion. Additionally, I have experience with designing and creating multiple websites for both personal and client purposes.

I am extremely passionate about creating awareness through design and really love bringing characters and emotion to life through my work. I always enjoy learning new techniques to implement into my next project. Should you have any interest in my work, would like to collaborate, or just say hello, feel free to contact me!
